ABOUT BLISSFUL RHYTHM

Blissful Rhythms is a collective comprised of individuals from diverse backgrounds brought together by a vision of using music to improve the surroundings in the community and positively affect the way people see themselves, their lives and their possibilities. In this pursuit for community empowerment, the collective realized that there is a gap in the market to provide good quality events that would stimulate youth engagement through music and fashion, and place emphasis on positive behaviors whilst providing a form of entertainment.
Youth - Music - Fashion
